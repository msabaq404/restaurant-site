Our Story

Savr was founded in 2010 by Chef Isabella Rossi, a culinary visionary with a deep-rooted passion for crafting unforgettable dining experiences. Inspired by her global travels and her rich Italian heritage, Isabella envisioned a place that would celebrate not only the art of cooking, but also the joy of gathering and sharing meaningful moments over delicious meals.

From its humble beginnings as a small neighborhood eatery, Savr has blossomed into a renowned culinary destination. Over the years, it has become known for its bold, innovative dishes, warm and inviting ambiance, and consistently exceptional service. Our unwavering commitment to sourcing the freshest local ingredients and our dedication to culinary excellence have earned us both critical acclaim and a loyal community of food lovers.

At Savr, we believe that every meal should be a celebration — a moment to pause, connect, and enjoy. Whether you're joining us for an intimate dinner, a lively family gathering, or a casual lunch with friends, we strive to create an experience that excites the senses and creates lasting memories. Our menu features an inspired variety of dishes, from timeless Italian classics to bold, contemporary creations, each crafted with precision, care, and a deep respect for flavor.

We warmly invite you to explore our story, discover our passion for food, and be part of the journey that makes Savr unique. Join us in a culinary adventure that promises to tantalize your taste buds, awaken your senses, and nourish your soul. Welcome to Savr — where every dish is a masterpiece, and every guest is part of our extended family.
